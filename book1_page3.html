He came up with a plan. He made some noise for the demons to get out and he entered from the backdoor. When he saw his buddies he almost cried, but he had to keep his composure. He took the vial they used for the ritual, because it contained their blood. As he was trying to leave the house from the backdoor a demon saw him from the right side and began to chase him. Multiple demons were chasing Jack until he was surrounded by them. “This is the end” – Jack thought, when he heard gunshots and one of the demons got murdered. The rest recognized the person responsible for killing their partner and in fear they ran away. Her name was Cassandra.
