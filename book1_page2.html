After a while he found the page that said a demon can be put to rest by the blood of those who summoned him. He began to think how to get back to that place. “Probably the demon was waiting for him” – that was the thought he had when he heard a strange noise coming from the front door. It was one of the demons spawn that came after him. He had no choice, but to flee once again and he returned safely to the house where him and his friends summoned the great demon. Jack saw from the windows that the demon was not there only its spawn protecting the dead bodies of his friends. There was no choice, he had to get inside somehow.
